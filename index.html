What it does

A companion for the journey — and the days after.

From the moment your group lands to the daily prayer years later. Click any feature to explore it.

Live group map Free

Mapbox-powered "Emerald Cockpit" map with real-time member positions, proximity zones, meeting points and offline pins. Built on Firebase Realtime Database for instant updates.

Quran reader, daily Free

All 114 surahs offline, tajweed colouring, audio recitation by world-class qaris, bookmarks, last-read tracking, and shareable ayah cards. Build a reading streak that lasts beyond your trip.

Prayer times & adhan Free

Exact-alarm prayer scheduling, multiple calculation methods, Hanafi Asr support, adhan sounds. Notifications fire even when your phone is asleep.

Smart bill split Paid

Snap a receipt — Gemini Vision OCR reads it (tuned for Arabic and bilingual Gulf receipts), Tabscanner and ML Kit handle fallback & offline. Equal or per-item split, payment tracking, cent-accurate UID-keyed maths.

Group chat & calls Paid

Text, images, voice notes, emoji, typing indicators — and admin tools for join requests, approvals and polite group-switch flows. Free members can read; Paid members can speak.

Help a Lost Pilgrim Paid

Found someone lost with no phone? Enter their group code and see their group live on a map. Translation in 15+ languages — reunite a stranger with their family in minutes. Wasl's signature feature.

SOS & low-battery alerts Paid

Data-only FCM with full-screen lock-screen alerts. At 5% battery Wasl automatically broadcasts your zone, hotel, and meeting points. Three-layer background tracking keeps you findable overnight.

99 Names & Quran Stories Free

Daily reflections on Allah's names with audio, plus bite-sized Quran Stories. Tuesday and Friday nudges keep you engaged year-round — not just during your trip.

Hotel pin & search Paid

Mapbox geocoding finds and pins your accommodation. Share it with your group, return to it from anywhere in the city. Especially helpful when your phone keyboard is in a different language.

Helper-tracking protection Coming soon

When someone uses Lost Pilgrim Helper to locate you, your group gets notified with a blinker dot and a one-tap block. Privacy by design, rolling out after the current Hajj test window.

Emerald Cockpit map

Real-time member positions on a beautifully styled Mapbox base. Proximity zones, meeting points, offline pins. Powered by Firebase RTDB so locations update instantly across every device in the group.

The Wasl difference

We won't pretend GPS works where it doesn't.

Inside the Haram, satellite signals bounce off marble and steel. Most apps fake it. Wasl was designed to adapt — three honest modes, automatically chosen by signal quality.

i.

Pinpoint

Outside the Haram, full GPS precision via Mapbox. See your group's exact position on a real map — gates, plazas, hotels, transit.

Outdoor accuracy
ii.

Zone

Inside the Haram, fuzzy polygons highlight which area your group is in — Mataf North, Sa'i Middle, Clock Tower lobby. A zone wedge keeps you oriented.

Indoor honesty
iii.

Last Seen

Signal lost? Wasl shows the last verified location and time. Set meeting points in advance — and the auto-SOS handles the rest.

Failsafe
