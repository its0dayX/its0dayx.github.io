What it does

Everything your group needs, nothing it doesn't.

From the moment you land to the moment you return home — Wasl handles the chaos so you can focus on your worship.

Group tracking Free

Three honest modes: Pinpoint outdoors, Zone inside the Haram, Last Seen when signals drop. No false confidence — real coordination.

Smart bill split Paid

OCR scans your receipt, splits per item, and locks the currency rate at the moment of purchase. Settle later in any currency without surprises.

Lost Pilgrim Helper Paid

Find an elderly pilgrim with no phone? Wasl translates instantly into their language, finds their group across the city, and reunites them in minutes.

Crowd awareness Free

Live density signals across the Mataf, Sa'i, and prayer halls. Know when to go, when to wait, and which gate is least crowded right now.

Umrah Guide & Du'as Free

Step-by-step rituals — Tawaf, Sa'i, Halq, Ihram — with authenticated du'as in Arabic, transliteration, and your language. Always offline.

Low-battery SOS Paid

When your phone hits 5%, Wasl pings your group with your last known zone, your hotel, and your meeting points — automatically.

Pinpoint, Zone, Last Seen

Other apps lie about indoor GPS. We don't. Wasl uses pre-mapped zones inside the Haram so you always know roughly where your group is — even in the densest crowds.

The Wasl difference

We won't pretend GPS works where it doesn't.

Inside the Haram, satellite signals bounce off marble and steel. Most apps fake it. We designed three modes that adapt to reality.

i.

Pinpoint

Outside the Haram, full GPS precision. See your group's exact position on a real map — gates, plazas, hotels, transit.

Zone
